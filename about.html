2014:

Square Eyes Launches

Square Eyes, an innovative online movie renting service, is founded by a group of tech-savvy film enthusiasts.

2015:

First Content Licensing Deals

Square Eyes secures its first content licensing agreements with several major film studios, allowing them to offer a wide selection of movies.

2016:

Mobile App Launch

Square Eyes launches its mobile app, making it more convenient for users to rent and stream movies on the go.

2017

Original Content Production

Square Eyes ventures into original content production, releasing its first exclusive movie, "Pixel Dreams," to critical acclaim.

2018

International Expansion

Square Eyes expands its services to international markets, offering multi-language support and regional content.

2020

VR Integration

Square Eyes integrates virtual reality (VR) technology, allowing users to experience select movies in an immersive, 3D environment.

2021

Personalized Recommendation Algorithm

The company introduces a cutting-edge recommendation algorithm, using machine learning to suggest movies tailored to each user's preferences.

2022

Partnerships with Independent Filmmakers

Square Eyes collaborates with independent filmmakers, showcasing indie films and providing a platform for emerging talent.

2023

4K Streaming and Smart TV Integration

Square Eyes enhances its streaming quality to 4K resolution and integrates seamlessly with smart TVs, providing the ultimate cinematic experience at home. The company continues to dominate the online movie rental industry with a vast library of content, innovative technology, and a growing user base.
